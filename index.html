About Me

Having worked in a variety of technology support roles in the higher education field for well over a decade I have the innate ability to pick up things quickly and move from one project to another with ease. As I transition my skills from support to building, I have poured my energy into learning new languages and frameworks to build exciting and robust applications.

My career has taken me to the University of Kentucky, University of South Carolina, and now living in Charlotte, North Carolina. Having spent a great deal of time in learning technology support systems and video stream and conferncing systems, I am excited to transition these skills into web devlopment.

Now in Charlotte, I have the opportunity to work at a brewery a hobby I have had for about 6 years now. I have been able to learn a great deal in small business operations, a significant change from the University environment I have previously been used to.

Another hobby I have developed since moving to the Carolinas has been cycling, both in the activity and mechanics. What started off an interest in cycle-commuting developed into participating in the creation of a community bicycle shop. Learning to work on bicycles and assist members of the community who rely on bicycles as their main source of transportation.
